  • A7:Slide down the top of the screen, click “Switch”, then you can find a battery icon with the percentage of its remaining life.

  • Q11:How should I charge my new phone?
  • A11:For the first time, you should leave your phone to run out the battery, and then turn off the phone and recharge it with Infinix charger for 8 hours.
  • Q12:Google play store pictures don't appear in my phone.
  • A12:Please try to clear Google play cache.
  • Q13:How to add Arabic language in mobile's keyboard?
  • A13:Add Arabic language or any language else you need to open SwiftKey -> setting -> languages -> search any language you want to add -> download it. Please note that before download any language, you must enable the Internet connection first.
