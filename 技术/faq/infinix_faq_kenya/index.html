  • Q11:How should I charge my new phone?
  • A11:For the first time, you should leave your phone to run out the battery, and then turn off the phone and recharge it with Infinix charger for 8 hours.
  • Q12:Google play store pictures don't appear in my phone.
  • A12:Please try to clear Google play cache.
  • Q13:How to add another language in your mobile's keyboard?
  • A13:To add any language you need to open SwiftKey -> setting -> languages -> search any language you want to add -> download it. Please note that before download any language, you must enable the Internet connection first.
