How to be our supplier?

We always puts priority on quality of services, that’s why we are looking forward to cooperate with the best supplier who is able to tie with international rivals and pursue sustainable development as well. Our suppliers gave us a great support by providing innovative products, reliable services, on time delivery, and the market pricing, etc. In return, we have established a long-term cooperative partnership of equality and mutual benefit with them.

 

 

Do you want to be supplier of TRANSSION HOLIDNGS? If you meet the basic requirements below, you are welcome to send the information of your company to this email address purchase@transsion.com.

 

Basic requirements for the product and brand:

 

1. Have a good brand reputation and certain popularity in the industry;

2.  Excellent product quality; and manufacturers should have strict product quality control system;

3. Manufacturer should have perfect sale and after-sale service;

4. Products are required to meet all kinds of mandatory standard (we require the certificates of CE, ROSH, etc.).

 

 

Basic requirements to suppliers:

1. Provide a competitive price and policies to guarantee sufficient capacity and stable supply;

2.  Accept payment on term or bank acceptance;

3.  Agencies need to provide the valid authorization files of the product, while manufacturers need to provide the trademark registration of its products

4. Offer valid company documents (such as business license, tax registration certificate, and organization code certificate);

5.  Have the general taxpayer qualification; for all the supply of products, can provide value-added tax invoices, the content of which should be in consistent with the actual purchase goods;

6.  Provide a good support of after-sale service.
