TECNO links products' sale to innovation

Tecno Mobile has said that its products have been accepted in the competitive African mobile phone market due to its innovations in phone making.

The company said though successful brands in the global market shared one key element of brand development, it was not left out in the universal trend having been at the forefront of“high quality product offerings, affordability and improvement.”

This feat, it said in a statement it made available to our correspondent, “has in no small measure helped us to meet the growing needs of African markeIn making customer satisfaction the highest measure of quality, Tecno said it had not only set the benchmark in the technology sector, but had also contributed immensely to the social and economic development of the countries where it operated.

The mobile phone maker said it understood that the continuous improvement of product image and brand value was very vital, not only to the survival of the brand, but also to its resounding success in the ever-evolving mobile technology space and a highly competitive market.

“Tecno mobile continues to strive for positive change in delivering world-class quality, yet making it accessible to everyone, no matter the age or class.

“This is clearly evident in the line of products and after-sales service Tecno mobile offers to its massive customer base.

“Little wonder why Tecno mobile has received this global quality recognition award,” the statement further read.

Recently, the President of Business Initiative Directions, Mr. Jose Prieto, presented the Quality Crown award, Gold category, to the Vice President of Tecno Mobile Limited, Mr. Mohammad Chowdhury.

The BID International quality crown award acknowledges strong commitment of global companies to quality and excellence.

More so, at the just concluded London 2014 presentation of awards, Tecno Mobile was among companies from 51 countries, including MindZet -Denmark; Zurich Sigorta -Turkey; Agricultural Development Bank – Ghana; AL-Watania Poultry – Saudi Arabia; Newswatch – Nigeria, among others that received awards in different categories.

With this development, Tecno now joins the league of BID winners for commitment to quality and customer satisfaction having met the Quality Control 100 criteria of BID’s Total Quality Management.

Other renowned world businesses that have joined the league include TATA Group; Turner construction (builders of the world’s tallest building, BurjKhalifa); Inventec Electronics; Alcoa Hownet Gennevilliers; to name a few.

Speaking at the award ceremony, Prieto said, “The award recipients are icons of commitment to leadership, technology and innovation, making them models for others in their sectors.”

In his reaction while receiving the award, Chowdhury said, “We make quality a core focus for continuous improvement of management to maintain 997 leadership in our sector.”

According to him, Tecno Mobile as the fastest growing mobile brand in Africa has continued to show innovation in design, quality and product pricing with research and development centre in France.

He said that Tecno Mobile introduced the first dual SIM mobile phones to Africa and now enjoyed huge patronage across the continent in just five years.

He assured subscribers that Tecno would continue to manufacture high performance feature phones and smartphones with recreational functions and “seamless Internet connectivity sold at affordable prices with +two years warranty across support centres.”

Similarly, Tecno was also recently awarded the international Quality Crown award in the Gold category. It was recognised for quality, leadership, technology and innovation at the BID Quality Convention in London.
